## Current Projects:
### Senior Speech Bingo
This is a web app version of a game the marching band trumpet section at my university plays during senior speeches. Prior to this, we would
have to make the boards by hand, and the entire section shared the same board. This one randomizes boards from a pre-defined selection of
tiles so that every person gets a unique board.

The visual design isn't great, but that's because 1) I suck at it, 2) it's a niche enough of a use that I didn't care past basic visuals, and 3) I didn't spend that much time making it anyway.
The background image used is a picture of our band director, and was added as a visual gag and inside joke.
