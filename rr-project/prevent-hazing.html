How can you take action against hazing?

What Hazing Looks Like:

Hazing takes on many forms, such as:
-Intimidation (deception, isolation, threats, demeaning acts)
-Harrassment (verbal abuse, threats, humiliation, sleep-deprivation)
-Violence (forced consumption of substances, physical assault, sexual assault)

Preventing Hazing:

-Understand what hazing looks like
-Take courses/trainings on hazing information and prevention (especially if your institution/organization has their own)
-Know how to respond to and report a hazing incident

Responding to Hazing:

-Get help from another another person, whether it's a friend, another student/member, or a organization staff member.
-File a hazing report to your institution/organization
-In case of imminent danger to a person, call 911
